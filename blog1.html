A small brief information about classes and objects.


Dec 1,2020 . 11:50pm

There are lots of object-oriented languages that students find difficult to understand the first time they read. So here short information that leads you to a better understanding of the classes and objects concept.

In the real world, we can see many objects around us such as cars, mobile, pets, humans, etc. All these objects have some state and behaviour(functions). lets me take an example.

Suppose, you would buy a new laptop then what will its states and behaviours?

Its states would be the brand name, boot speed, battery backup and the behaviour would be like playing games, playing movies, sleep mode, power ON and OFF, etc.

So, here the laptop is class.

brandName -String, bootSpeed -int, batteryBackup -int. All these are states that are the field.

playGame(), playMovie(), sleepMode(). All these are functions.
